# The Transformation Shift Pods progress through a structured five-stage Pod Lifecycle where stage gates and feedback loops drive focus, rigor and impact

*For Internal Use – For Internal Distribution Only. Updated from 22nd June 2026 GLT Cadence.*

Feedback loop: if a hypothesis is invalidated at Stage 3, the Pod refines back through Stage 2 or Stage 1. If scaled impact is not sustained after Stage 4, it may trigger a new Problem Framing cycle (back to Stage 1).

---

## Stage 1 — Problem Framing

- **Accountability (in alignment with GLT Sponsor(s)):** Pod Champion (in alignment with Functional Owner)
- **Estimated Duration:** ~2.5 – 3 weeks

**Definition of Done (DoD)**
1. **Problem Statement (PS) Identification:** identify potential pain points, assumed root cause, preliminary future state and success metrics
2. **Problem Validation:** stress-test with evidence, confirm root cause, future state and success metrics

**Stage Gate Outputs — Pod Charter**
1. Problem Statement – Future State – Success Metrics
2. Success Metrics baseline
3. OKR value linkage*

**Stage Gates**
- 22 Jun — SCo Kick-off: Pod Progress Update
- 6 Jul — SCo #1: Pod Progress Update

---

## Stage 2 — Solution Framing

- **Accountability (in alignment with GLT Sponsor(s)):** Pod Champion (in alignment with Functional Owner)
- **Estimated Duration:** ~3–4 weeks

**Definition of Done (DoD)**
1. **Solution Identification:** identify potential solutions and hypotheses, and assess feasibility
2. **Minimum Viable Solution (MVS) Design**
3. **Pilot Roll-out Plan**

**Stage Gate Outputs — MVS Roll-out Plan**
1. Solution design (target user, specification of solution, benefits)
2. Solution roll-out plan (timeline, stakeholders, effectiveness measures)

**Stage Gates**
- Est. 23 Jul — SCo #3: approve Pod Charter & MVS Roll-out Plan

---

## Stage 3 — Pilot & Refine

- **Accountability (in alignment with GLT Sponsor(s)):** Pod Champion (in alignment with Functional Owner)
- **Estimated Duration:** ~12 – 15 weeks

**Definition of Done (DoD)**
1. **Pilot Execution:** run the pilot, track effectiveness, capture learnings and refine progressively
2. **Hypothesis and scalability validation:** measure pilot against success metrics to validate hypothesis and scalability

*Note: if invalidated, refine through Stage 2 or Stage 1.*

**Stage Gate Outputs — Pilot Report**
1. Pilot effectiveness against planned measures
2. Recommendation to scale

**Stage Gates**
- 13 Aug — SCo #4: Pod Progress Update
- 24 Sep – 26 [date] — SCo #5: Pod Progress Update
- Est. 27 Oct — approve Pilot Report

---

## Stage 4 — Adoption & Scale

- **Accountability (in alignment with GLT Sponsor(s)):** Pod Champion → Functional Owner
- **Estimated Duration:** ~2–3 weeks

**Definition of Done (DoD)**
1. **Solution Handover:** Pod Champion to transition all stage gate outputs to Functional Owner
2. **Enterprise Rollout, Adoption & Monitoring:** Functional Owner to develop and execute Enterprise roll-out & adoption plan as well as monitor success metrics

*Note: upon scaling, if impact is not sustained, potential new Problem Framing.*

**Stage Gate Outputs — Handover Report**
1. Stage gate outputs
2. Enterprise roll-out plan

**Stage Gates**
- Est. 18 Nov — SCo #7: approve Handover Report

---

## Stage 5 — Embed & Sustain

- **Accountability (in alignment with GLT Sponsor(s)):** Functional Owner
- **Estimated Duration:** ~Continuous improvement

**Definition of Done (DoD)**
1. **Function integration:** embed solution into daily operations, and standardise process
2. **Continuous improvement:** monitor long-term effectiveness, optimise based on evolving needs and measure impact

**Stage Gate Outputs**
*Note: continuous improvement by Functional Owner.*
