This leads to significant threat to talent retention and capability to deliver projects.

### Overall Conclusion

This evidence confirms talent seek clarity on career development framework with structured and measurable competency pathways rather than focusing on compensation and other perks. This supports that majority of the talent understand and are aware of the company's strategic growth target and how their role plays its part to achieve the target.

## Slide 3 — Gentari's Talent Paradox: High Mission Commitment vs. Critical Enablement Gaps

**Mission Alignment is Strong**

- **87%** of employees understand how their role contributes to Gentari's success.
- **85%** intend to stay with Gentari over the next 12 months.

**Career Infrastructure is Weak**

- **66%** of employees operate without an active Individual Development Plan (IDP).
- **7%** are extremely confident in the organization's development support.
- **54%** signal a critical demand for a clear career roadmap.

> Employees are not asking for more perks. They are asking for visibility, progression, development, and faster organizational execution.

## Slide 7 — Pod Delivery View

### Problem Statement

Talent growth and retention are constrained (issue) due to insufficient structured development and unclear capability pathways (root cause) leading to a weakened talent pipeline and reduced organisational capability (impact) to deliver $613 million EBITDA target for 2030.

### Blockers & Risk

**Active Blockers**
- Existing talent development template and tools are not fully distributed and accessible to BU outside of Malaysia, especially to employees that are not under the PETRONAS email domain.

**Emerging Risks**
- Potential increase in critical talent attrition due to unclear career pathways.
- Delays in technical competency framework implementation impacting workforce readiness.
- Insufficient leadership pipeline and succession readiness for future growth.

### Support Needed

- ICT intervention is required to enable distribution of information and internal job availability postings to every entity within Gentari.
- Potential consultancy engagement and budget allocation are required to develop structured and measurable technical competency pathways/framework to ensure effective assessment of talent.
